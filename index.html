Research Focus

Areas of Expertise

01
Spectral & Pseudospectral Methods
Development of Legendre and Chebyshev pseudospectral discretization schemes for high-accuracy approximation of time-dependent PDEs.
02
Nonlinear Evolution Equations
Numerical analysis of multidimensional Burgers, Burgers–Fisher, and generalized nonlinear parabolic equations with steep gradients and discontinuities.
03
Fractional Differential Equations
Spectral approximations for fractional PDEs including fractional cable and fractional Burgers equations based on the Caputo fractional derivative.
04
Filter-Enhanced Collocation
Design of fast algorithms with spectral filtering for polynomial collocation methods addressing non-smooth solutions with discontinuous behavior.
05
Computational Fluid Dynamics
Application of high-order numerical schemes to quasi-linear fluid dynamics problems, including robust solvers for convection-dominated flows.
06
Mathematical Modelling
Oscillation dynamics in ecological metapopulation models, including Allee effect studies and bifurcation analysis in coupled nonlinear systems.

I welcome research collaborations, visiting positions, and discussions on spectral methods for challenging PDE problems.
